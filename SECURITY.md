# Security Policy

We take the security of Whisper AI very seriously.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it through the form on our help page: https://usewhisper.org/help

### What to include in your report

- Description of the vulnerability
- Potential impact or severity
- Steps to reproduce
- Affected versions/components
- Hardware & software information

### What to expect

Once you submit a report, we'll review it and take suitable action. We may reach out to discuss further details and will keep you updated on the status of the issue.

### Recognition

As a thank you for helping us improve the security of Whisper AI, we offer optional credit in our contributor list for valid vulnerability reports.
